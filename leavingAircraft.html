LEAVING THE AIRCRAFT

  • WHEELS CHOCKS.......IN PLACE
  • YELLOW BRAKE PRESSURE.......VERIFY 3000 PSI
  • PARKING BRAKE (IF BRAKE TEMPS BELOW 300°C).......ON
  • (IF BRAKE TEMPS ABOVE 300°C).......OFF
  • CREW O2.......OFF
  • ADIRS ROTARY SELECTORS.......OFF
  • EXTERIOR LIGHTS.......OFF
  • EMERGENCY EXIT LIGHTS.......OFF
  • CABIN PRESSURE MODE PUSHBUTTON.......AUTO
  • EXT POWER.......AS REQUIRED
  • FUEL PUMPS.......OFF
  • APU BLEED PUSHBUTTON.......OFF
  • MCDU.......FULL DIM
  • APU MASTER PUSHBUTTON.......OFF

AFTER APU SHUTDOWN

  • ECAM.......FULL DIM
  • RMP 1 & 2.......OFF
  • ACP 1 & 2.......OFF
  • BAT PUSHBUTTONS.......OFF WHEN APU FLAP CLOSES
  • EXT POWER.......OFF

YOU MUST WAIT 90 SECONDS BEFORE TURNING OFF BATTERY PUSHBUTTONS TO ALLOW PROPER TIME FOR APU INLET TO CLOSE
