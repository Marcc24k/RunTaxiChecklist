ENGINE FIRE ON GROUND

MECHANICS A320 FAMILY EMERGENCY QUICK REFERENCE CHECKLIST

  • THRUST LEVERS.......IDLE
  • AIRCRAFT.......STOP / HOLD POSITION
  • PARKING BRAKE.......ON
  • MASTER WARNING PUSHBUTTON.......PRESS
  • ENG MASTER SWITCH (AFFECTED ENGINE).......OFF
  • ENGINE FIRE PUSHBUTTON (AFFECTED ENGINE).......RELEASE
  • WAIT 10 SECS FOR FIRE TO EXTINGUISH.......OBSERVE

IF ENGINE FIRE CONTINUES

  • AGENT 1.......DISHCARGE
  • ATC / GROUND CONTROL.......NOTIFY
  • WAIT 30 SECS FOR FIRE TO EXTINGUISH.......OBSERVE

IF ENGINE FIRE CONTINUES

  • AGENT 2.......DISHCARGE
  • WAIT 30 SECS FOR FIRE TO EXTINGUISH.......OBSERVE
  • ATC / GROUND CONTROL.......NOTIFY

IF ENGINE FIRE IS UNEXTINGUISHABLE, AND AIRCRAFT EVACUATION REQUIRED:

  • ATC/ GROUND CONTROL.......NOTIFY
  • ENGINE MASTER SWITCH (OPPSITE SIDE).......OFF
  • FUEL PUMPS.......OFF
  • APU MASTER SWITCH.......OFF
  • BAT PUSHBUTTON 1+2 (IF TIME PERMITS).......OFF
  • EVACUATION PROCEDURES.......INITIATE
