BEFORE ENGINE START (V2500)

  • ENGINE FIRE PUSHBUTTON 1 & 2.......IN / GUARDED
  • ENG 1 & 2 FIRE TEST.......PERFORM
  • THRUST REVERSERS.......IDLE
  • BEACONS.......ON
  • FUEL PUMPS.......ON
  • GROUND CLEARANCE ( CALL RAMP IF REQUIRED).......OBTAIN

STARTER DUTY-CYCLE LIMITATIONS(V2500)

DO NOT ENGAGE STARTER AGAIN WITH ENGINE MOTORING ABOVE 10% N2 - DOING SO WILL DAMAGE THE START SHAFT

    A- THREE ENGINE STARTING ATTEMPTS- STARTUP TO IDLE

  • 1st START ATTEMPT-IF FAIL, 15 SEC COOL DOWN
  • 2nd START ATTEMPT-IF FAIL, 15 SEC COOL DOWN
  • 3rd START ATTEMPT-IF FAIL, 30 MIN COOL DOWN

    • B- THREE ENGINE CRANKING SESSIONGS - CRANKING ONLY

  • 2 MIN CRANK - 15 SEC COOL DOWN AFTER N2 = 0%
  • 2 MIN CRANK - 15 SEC COOL DOWN AFTER N2 = 0%
  • 1 MIN CRANK - 30 MIN COOL DOWN

    • C- ONE ENGINE CRANKING SESSIONGS - CRANKING ONLY

  • 4 MIN CRANK - 30 MIN COOL DOWN
