BEFORE ENGINE START (PW1100)

  • ENGINE FIRE PUSHBUTTON 1 & 2.......IN / GUARDED
  • ENG 1 & 2 FIRE TEST.......PERFORM
  • THRUST REVERSERS.......IDLE
  • BEACONS.......ON
  • FUEL PUMPS.......ON
  • DUEL COOLING PUSHBUTTON.......OFF
  • GROUND CLEARANCE ( CALL RAMP IF REQUIRED).......OBTAIN

STARTER DUTY-CYCLE LIMITATIONS (PW1100)

DO NOT ENGAGE STARTER AGAIN WITH ENGINE MOTORING ABOVE 1 % N2 - MUST REACH 0% N2

EEC WILL COMMAND STARTER AIR VALVE CLOSED IF DUTY CYCLE IS EXCEEDED

    STARTER DUTY CYCLE:

  • 3 START ATTEMPTS - 35 SECONDS COOLING PERIOD BETWEEN EACH, FOLLOWED BY A 15 MINUTE COOL DOWN
  • 1 CONTINUOUS CRANK UP TO 15 MINUTES, FOLLOWED BY A 15 MINUTE COOL DOWN

STARTER DUTY-CYCLE LIMITATIONS (PW1100)

  • COOLING TIME IS DISPLAYED ON UPPER ECAM IF EEC DETERMINES ENGINE MOTORING IS REQUIRED FOR BOWED ROTOR PROTECTION. ENGINE MOTORS FOR DISPLAYED TIME BEFORE START SEQUENCE BEGINS (APPROX 8 - 10% N2)
